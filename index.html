MY PROJECT

Featured Project

SIAFARM (E-Commerce)

This project showcases the mobile UI/UX design of SIAFARM, an agricultural e-commerce platform developed to connect local farmers with consumers through a simple, accessible, and user-friendly digital experience. The interface is designed with a clean green-based visual identity to reflect freshness and sustainability, supported by intuitive navigation across key features such as product browsing, category filters, user authentication, shopping cart management, and order tracking. Each screen prioritises clarity and usability—ranging from the onboarding and registration flow to detailed product pages and the checkout process—ensuring users can easily explore agricultural products and complete transactions with minimal friction. Throughout the design process, I applied wireframing, usability considerations, and a user-centred approach to deliver an efficient, aesthetically consistent, and functionally reliable platform tailored for both farmers and buyers.

North Toraja Tourism

This design showcases a modern and visually engaging tourism landing page for the Government of North Toraja Regency, built to highlight the region’s cultural richness and natural attractions. The layout features a prominent hero section with a traditional Tongkonan background image, complemented by administrative information placed in a subtle overlay for clarity. A clean two-column structure presents key content such as the achievements of Londorundun Tourism Village and the unique landscape of Gumuk Pasir Sumalu, supported by high-quality photos that emphasise authenticity and local identity. The colour palette blends maroon accents, natural greens, and soft gradients to create a warm yet professional feel, while clear visual hierarchy ensures easy navigation and readability. The footer consolidates social media links, contact details, and the government’s address within a curved red background, adding a modern touch that balances the overall composition. This design effectively combines aesthetics, functionality, and regional storytelling to deliver an informative and immersive tourism experience.

Gammara Village Web

This design showcases a structured and user-friendly community information portal for Gammara Village, created as part of a university project to demonstrate practical web interface development. The layout features a welcoming hero banner that introduces the village, supported by clear navigation menus for profiles, services, information, and local businesses. Key sections such as Latest News, Announcements, and Activity Agenda are organised in a clean grid system to ensure easy access to village updates. The colour scheme uses green as the primary identity tone, reinforcing a natural and approachable visual theme. The footer includes essential village details, contact information, and project attribution, creating a complete and professional presentation suitable for official community use.

Social Media Admin & Designer - PS Morowa

This project showcases my role as both designer and digital media administrator for PS Morowa, an amateur football club, where I developed visually engaging match-day posters to strengthen the team’s branding and public communication. The designs incorporate bold typography, dynamic player photography, and team colours to create a cohesive visual identity across regular match promotions and final match announcements. Each poster layout is crafted to highlight key match information—such as date, venue, and opposing team—while maintaining a professional and energetic aesthetic suited for social media distribution. As the club’s admin, I managed content scheduling, coordinated with players and team staff for accurate data, and ensured consistent visual communication across all digital platforms. This work demonstrates my ability to combine creative design, brand identity development, and effective media management for a community-based sports organisation.
